SnT Winter Camp'19 Recruitment

Come join us!!


As the winter is in the corner and the semester is nearing its end, we bring to you the 'SnT Winter Camp 2019'.
Science and Technology Council utilizes the Winter Vacation as a time period to recruit a few students and help them learn about various topics in varied domains. This is a time when the clubs attempt to boost your learning curve through a very comprehensive set of lectures, workshops and projects.
We are hosting a lecture today to let you know about the Winter Camp plans of various clubs and what the selection policy shall be.
Date: 22nd of October, 2019
Time: 8:00 PM - 9:30 PM
Venue: L-18
